Crosshatch design tool

This design tool creates 3d food printable crosshatch patterns.
Designing your own is easy, select the number of columns, rows, layers and the width and length.
Click on the settings button to fine tune your 3d food printer parameters.
Once you have design that you love and the settings right click save to SAVE your printable file.
